Top 5 Tips to Book Cheap Flights

Find out how to save big on airfare with these expert tips.

1. Be Flexible With Your Travel Dates

Flexibility is the golden rule when booking cheap flights. If you can shift your travel dates by just a few days, you might save thousands of rupees. Flying mid-week is often cheaper than weekends.

2. Use Incognito Mode While Searching

Ever noticed prices increasing after a few searches? Travel websites track your visits using cookies. Always use incognito or private browsing mode to get the best prices.

3. Set Fare Alerts

Use flight comparison tools to set fare alerts. These alerts notify you when prices drop for your desired route. This saves time and ensures you book at the best price.

4. Consider Budget Airlines

Budget carriers often offer better deals, especially for domestic routes. Just ensure you’re aware of their baggage policies and other fees before booking.

5. Book in Advance (But Not Too Early)

While booking early can secure low fares, booking too early may lead to missing out on discounts. The sweet spot is usually 1–3 months in advance for domestic and 2–5 months for international travel.
